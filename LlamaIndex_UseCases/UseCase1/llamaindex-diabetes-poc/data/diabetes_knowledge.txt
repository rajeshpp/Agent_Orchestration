Type 2 Diabetes is a chronic condition where the body does not use insulin properly.
Symptoms include frequent urination, fatigue, blurred vision and increased thirst.
Lifestyle interventions include diet control, physical exercise and weight loss.
HbA1c value above 6.5% indicates diabetes and 5.7%-6.4% indicates prediabetes.
