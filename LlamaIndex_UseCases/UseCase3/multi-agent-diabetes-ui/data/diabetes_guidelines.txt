Type 2 Diabetes diagnostic criteria include HbA1c ≥ 6.5%, FPG ≥ 126 mg/dL, or 2-hour OGTT ≥ 200 mg/dL. Lifestyle interventions include 150+ minutes/week moderate activity, weight loss 5–10%, and a focus on low-GI, high-fiber foods. Metformin is commonly first-line unless contraindicated; monitor eGFR. Screen for complications: retinopathy, neuropathy, CKD, ASCVD. Individualize targets and therapy.
